How to Cook

Cooking is a fundamental skill that anyone can learn with a few simple steps.

Follow these steps to start your cooking journey:

  • Gather all necessary ingredients and kitchen tools.
  • Prepare your ingredients by washing, chopping, and measuring them.
  • Follow a simple recipe, cooking each ingredient at the right time.
  • Season your dish to taste and serve it warm.
