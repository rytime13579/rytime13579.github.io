20 Tips for Staying Rich

Practical advice to keep your wealth growing and protected

1. Live below your means — spend less than you earn.
2. Diversify your investments to reduce risk.
3. Avoid high-interest debt at all costs.
4. Set financial goals and review them regularly.
5. Maintain an emergency fund with 3–6 months of expenses.
6. Invest in assets, not liabilities.
7. Continuously educate yourself about money and finance.
8. Hire financial advisors you trust — not just the cheapest.
9. Review and rebalance your portfolio annually.
10. Pay yourself first — save before you spend.
11. Avoid lifestyle inflation as your income grows.
12. Don’t chase trends; invest in long-term value.
13. Use tax-advantaged accounts to grow your wealth.
14. Protect your assets with insurance and estate planning.
15. Network with other financially successful people.
16. Keep detailed budgets and track your spending.
17. Be patient — compound interest rewards the disciplined.
18. Don’t make emotional decisions about money.
19. Have multiple income streams, not just one job.
20. Give back — generosity often brings opportunity and clarity.
